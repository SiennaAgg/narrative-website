About this website and the author

Hello, I'm Sienna, the proud cat parent behind this delightful corner of the internet dedicated to our feline friends! Since welcoming my kitten, Yuki, into my life in May 2023, I've experienced firsthand the incredible emotional support and joy that cats bring.

The Catalog is a heartfelt exploration of the world of cats, covering everything from comprehensive care tips and the joy of various breeds to the profound connection between cats and mental health. I've also included a blog so you can see my experience as a first time cat owner. Join me in celebrating the love and magic of cats, aiming to spread the joy they bring and help others discover how these whiskered companions can make life more fulfilling.

Whisker Magic

A cat's whiskers aren't just for show—they're highly sensitive! These amazing sensors help them navigate through tight spaces and judge whether they can fit into a particular space.

Tail Language

Cats use their tails to express themselves. A raised tail is a friendly greeting, while a puffed-up tail indicates fear or excitement. Paying attention to your cat's tail can provide insight into their mood.

Catnap Connoisseurs

Cats are master nappers, sleeping for an average of 12-16 hours a day! They're crepuscular, meaning they're most active during dawn and dusk, which leaves plenty of time for their favorite pastime—napping!

Kneading Experts

Ever wonder why your cat kneads you or their favorite blanket? This behavior, known as "kneading," is a throwback to kittenhood when they kneaded their mother's belly to stimulate milk flow.

Chirping Conversations

Cats have a unique communication style. When observing birds or prey, some cats make a chattering or chirping sound. It's believed to mimic the sound of birdsong, indicating their hunting instincts.

Tiny Acrobats

Kittens are born with an extraordinary sense of balance and coordination. In their first few weeks of life, these pint-sized acrobats can often be found playfully leaping, twisting, and somersaulting as they develop the agility they'll carry into adulthood. Enjoy the adorable antics of these miniature gymnasts!
