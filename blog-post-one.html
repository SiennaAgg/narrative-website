Growing Up Together

A month into my university life, and I can confidently declare this chapter as the absolute best time of my life. Yuki and I have seamlessly settled into our cozy flat. The transition to university has brought an unexpected joy – more time spent at home. Unlike the hustle and bustle of college life, Yuki and I are relishing in the newfound freedom. Gone are the days of constant monitoring due to an abundance of plants. Now, in our open flat, Yuki roams freely, claiming every nook as his personal kingdom.

In this new environment, Yuki's demeanor has undergone a delightful transformation. While he still holds onto his playful kitten spirit, he's also embraced the art of relaxation. Long naps during the day and cozy cuddles while binge-watching Netflix at night have become our shared rituals.

Considering Yuki's love for exploration, I'm contemplating adding a cat tree to our home. This way, when I'm off to lectures or social outings, he'll have his own playground to keep him entertained. A surefire way to ensure he's got something to do whenever the mood strikes.

Our days are filled with laughter and a touch of mischief. Yuki, the treat bandit, has developed a knack for stealing snack packets from the cupboards whenever hunger strikes. He even attempted to play hide-and-seek with my house keys in his litter tray before one of my lectures – a curious twist to my morning routine indeed.

And here's a quirky one – Yuki's newfound obsession with the fridge. Every time I open it, he's there, pushing past my hands and attempting to lay down inside as if he's found the gateway to feline paradise. Is this a common cat thing or just Yuki being his wonderfully peculiar self?

As mischievous escapades and cozy nights become the norm, I'm eagerly anticipating the countless memories yet to be made. Yuki and I have only just begun our journey together in this university adventure, and I can't wait to see what other antics and joyous moments await us.
